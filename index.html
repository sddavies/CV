Our expertise

Our in-house expertise covers a variety of different programming languages including SAP Netweaver ABAP/WebDynpro, C++, Java & Oracle PL/SQL.

We have worked with different database management systems ranging from corporate-level Oracle, used for large-scale transaction processing, down to single-user SQLite which we embed in our MetaTrader expert advisors to enrich functionality.

We also have experience of web technologies such as HTML, CSS, Javascript & XML

Finally, we are also familiar with MQL4. MQL4 is a derivate of C++ and is the core language for the MetaTrader 4 retail forex trading platform. The language is used to create add-ons and enhancements for the platform. Examples are Expert Advisors (for automated trading) and chart indicators (for trade signals).
