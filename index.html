Our purpose

Mykara is a software development consultancy.

We have over 30 years of software development experience.

Our goal is to help our clients to maximise growth potential and stand out from the crowd in an ever competitive world.

We do this by providing software services that put in place the IT solutions that our clients need in order for their businesses to flourish.

Our services

We offer a number of services to assist clients at any stage of the software development life-cycle:

  • Design
  • Development & Testing
  • Support & Training
